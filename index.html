Campfire

Here at Kadumane Hills Estate we are all about campfires. We think that camping without a campfire is totally crazy and that is why we encourage a safe fire in the evenings. Not only do campfires provide warmth and a focal point for any group or family, but loads of fun too!

We provide a fire pit to every group. We also provide benches and wooden logs around the fire pit so in the evenings you can sit comfortably and enjoy the warmth. At the same time respect the dangers associated with having campfires.

Here is a quick guide:
1. Only use the fire pits provided.
2. Fire pits should be at least 10 meters away from any tent.
3. Do not collect any wood yourselves!
4. Ashes and embers might still be hot in the morning.
5. Please do not burn anyother materials like paper and plastics in the fire pits.

Please note that the estate management cannot accept responsibility for your possessions or any personal injuries sustained while camping or using fires.
